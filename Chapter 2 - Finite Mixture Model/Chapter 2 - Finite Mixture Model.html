2.1 Finite Mixture Model

Gi s chng ta c

  • \(n\) quan trc (\(n\) hng), \(y_1, \ldots, y_n\)

  • Mi hng s c \(d\) chiu (\(d\) ct), : \(y_i=\left(y_{i, 1}, \ldots, y_{i, d}\right)\) .

A finite mixture model c nh ngha l:

  • Hm phn phi xc sut / Hm mt c lng \(p\) ca multivariate observation (quan trc a bin) \(y_i\),

  • c to t trung bnh trng s (weighted average) \(\tau\) ca \(G\) hm c lng xc sut thnh phn (density mixture component) \(f_g\).

\[ p\left(y_i\right)=\sum_{g=1}^G \tau_g f_g\left(y_i \mid \theta_g\right) . \tag{2.1} \]

Page 16 - Gii thch cng thc Finite Mixture Model

Trong :

  • \(\tau_g\) l xc sut observation thuc v component th \(g\)

  • \(\tau_g \geq 0\)

  • \(g=1, \ldots, G\),

  • \(\sum_{g=1}^G \tau_g=1\),

  • \(f_g\left(\cdot \mid \theta_g\right)\) l hm density ca component th \(g\) vi tham s l \(\theta_g\).

Vector trng s \(\tau\) y nhm mc ch: gi tr nm phn overlap gia 2 phn phi, trng s ca phn phi no cao hn s c kh nng thuc v phn phi ln hn.

Tham s \(\theta\) y l tham s ca phn phi. V d \(f(\ .\ |\ \theta\ )\) c phn phi chun th s c 2 tham s trung bnh v phng sai \(\mathcal N(\mu, \ \sigma^2)\)

2.2 Geometrically Constrained Multivariate Normal Mixture Models

Mi tham s ca m hnh cn c c lng ta xem n l mt bin cn lu. Cng thc tnh s tham s cn c lng nh sau:

\[ (G-1) + Gd + G\{d(d+1)/2\} \]

Trong :

  • \(G-1\): S lng tham s ca G-1 component, khng cn tnh ca G ci v ta c th t suy ra (ging vi bc t do).

  • \(Gd\): mi component c \(d\) chiu

  • \(G\{d(d+1)/2\}\): ma trn hip phng sai \((d \times d)\) tham s, nhng v n l ma trn i xng, nn ch cn (d(d + 1)/2) phn t duy nht.

V d:

  • Data faithful: \(d = \ 2,\ G = 2 \to\) 11 params.

  • \(\qquad\qquad\quad d = 27,\ G = 3 \to\) 1,217 params.

S lng ln params gy kh khn trong vic tnh ton ln biu din kt qu. Chim phn ln nht n t covariance matrix.

Do vy khc phc iu ny, ngi ta dng eigenvalue decomposition (hay Singular Value Decomposition) thay cho covariance matrix \(\Sigma_g\)

Page 20 - eigenvalue decomposition

\[ \Sigma_g = \lambda_gD_gA_gD_g^T. \tag{2.3} \] Trong : - \(\lambda_g\): vector t l th tch (Volume).

  • \(D_g\): ma trn eigenvalue th hin cho hng (Direction) phn tn ca cluster.

  • \(A_g\): ma trn ng cho eigenvalue. th hin cho ln phn tn.

Nu xt gia cc component khc nhau:

  • Nu \(\lambda_g\) l hng s, ngha l th tch ca cc component (cluster) bng nhau.

  • Nu mi component u c hng phn tn ging nhau mi Direction \(D_g = D\). Ta dng t Orientation ch iu ny.

  • Nu gi tr ca mi component u bng nhau \(A_g = A\) th ngha l mc phn tn ca n bng nhau mi hng.

Nu xt trong cng 1 component, ta ch quan tm n \(A\) v \(\lambda, D\) khng thay i, ngha l th tch v hng ging nhau nu xt trong cng 1 component.

  • Nu \(A_{1, g} > A_{2, g}\): ngha l mc phn tn theo direction 1 ln hn direction 2. Hay ni cch khc, cc im d liu trong cluster th \(g\) tp trung gn trc ng thng hng th 2 hn.

  • Nu \(A_{1, g} \approx A_{2, g} > A_{3, g}\) th component th \(g\) tp trung xung quanh hyperplane to bi direction 1 v 2.
